Delete this text and replace it Phone Troubleshooting Workflow

Greeting: Feel free to make your own. Here is a sample:

“Thank you for calling MadCap Technical Support, this is John. Who am I speaking with today?”

Note: Saying this not only introduces yourself to the customer properly but also gets the information from customer for us to verify that they are entitled to phone support. This is a perfect segue to the next topic.

Get Information:

• Name

• Email

• 5 consecutive characters of license key

Note: Before doing anything with customer, make sure that they are entitled for phone support.

Note: Have MadApps, TKB, Flare, Flare Help, Fogbugz, Bugbase, OSTicket open beforehand.

Setup Ticket:

• Make sure to log the time of call and take proper notes based on customer’s issue.

• If customer is new, make an OSTicket account for them.

• Make sure to fill out all the necessary fields on the ticket.

Ask for Issue:

• Take quality notes.

• Listen carefully and attentively.

• Pickup on keywords.

• Do not be afraid to ask for clarification if something is unclear.

Repeat back issue to customer.

This is to ensure to the customer that you understand the issue properly.

Note: Try to recreate the issue by just using your own machine before going in to Remote Session.

Team Viewer:

• URL to give to customers: remote.madcapsoftware.com

• Refer to this guide in terms of using TeamViewer: http://madnet/Content/TechSupport/Team%20Viewer%20Workflow.htm

Etiquette:

• Make sure to ask if you can take control before doing so.

• Try to work on your own screen especially if you are trying to locate a certain button or feature.

• BACKUP! BACKUP! BACKUP!

• Do not make any changes on their project/machine without customer permission or without making a backup first.

• Mute your headset if you are coughing, sighing, yawning or other things that you do that makes noise.

Troubleshooting:

• Start looking up knowledgebase and all sources.

• Always have a base to test. Go back to the basics of Flare and work your way up.

o Five core features of Flare: TOC, Topic, Target, Masterpage/Page Layout, Stylesheet

After research…

Issue solved:

Recap workflow verbally.

Ask customer if they have any other questions.

Send a recap summary of the call (Ticket you had setup).

Issue not solved:

Ask for project and work on it on your machine.

Send summary of call.

Ending Remarks:

Here is a sample:

Thank you for contacting us. If you have any other questions, feel free to call us again or file a ticket.

Have a great day!
